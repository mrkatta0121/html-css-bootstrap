    8 Charming Places To Visit In Vizag In Summer For Your Trip To Andhra Pradesh In 2022

    ..
    SHARES

    25 Oct 2021

    Written by

    Anjana (Guest Author)

    Vizag is the largest city of Andhra Pradesh and is known by many names owing to its popularity, some of them being ‘The City of Destiny’, ‘The Jewel of the East Coast’, and so on. Being a port city, it plays a big role in the economy of the country as well. It is situated in the Eastern Ghats and is a well-known tourist spot. Washed by the waves of the Bay of Bengal, this town has a number of tourist destinations and people come from far and wide to see all these places to visit in Vizag in summer. The city is well maintained, so if you are planning a trip here then you can go through the list given below about some of the best Vizag tourist places.

    Top 8 Places To Visit In Vizag In Summer, Andhra Pradesh

    Given below are 8 of the many charming places to visit in Visakhapatnam that you must not miss when youre visiting Andhra Pradesh and this city.

    1. INS Kurusura Submarine Museum

    Image Source

    The museum is an actual submarine and one of the best places to visit in Vizag in summer. It is situated on the Ramakrishna beach. Locally known as Smritika, this museum was earlier an INS Kursura, a Russian built submarine. In 2001, it was transformed into a submarine museum. This is one of its kind in entire Asia.

    The museum gives you a walk through the hardships of the warriors who are saving the nation. It depicts their lives through pictures and artifacts. This is definitely a place that you should not miss. It attracts a large number of tourists. This museum was inaugurated in august 2002 by Nara Chandrababu Naidu, former chief minister of Andhra Pradesh.

    Location: Ramakrishna beach
    Timings: 9 AM – 8 PM

    2. Matsyadarshini Aquarium

    Image Source

    An initiative of the municipal corporation of Visakhapatnam, this museum preserves saltwater and freshwater marine life. The museum is one of the most famous places to visit in Vizag in summer. Some of the fish that you are sure to see here are Clown Fish, Butterfly Fishes, Horn Cowfish, Damsels, Lionfish, LongHorn Cow Fish, Surgeon-Major, Butterfly Fishes, Lion Fish, Peacock Wrasse, Stone Fish, Red Squirrel Fish, Trigger Fish, Perch Dusky, Angels, Electric Ray, and many more. This is a completely air-conditioned aquarium managed by the municipal corporation.

    Location: Ramakrishna beach.
    Timings: 9 AM – 8 PM

    3. Araku Valley

    Sitting in the lap of nature Araku Valley is a must place to visit. There are a number of tribes living in this place. The Araku Valley also provides a home to a large number of tribal caves and the Tribal Art Museum. The exquisite coffee with its amazing aroma served here will have you craving for more. For those in love with adventure sports, this is just the place. You can also go for a trekking trip through the hills here. You are sure to have an amazing time at this place. Take the Vistadome train that will take you through 58 tunnels and over 84 bridges the sight is breathtaking.

    Best time to visit: October to March

    Suggested Read: Sea Ports In India: Know About The Important Ports Which Stands As A Glory Of The Country!

    4. Katiki Falls

    Image Source

    The Katiki falls is located on the river Gosthani. The waterfalls must be one of the top places in Vizag in summer for you to visit. This is a popular attraction for nature lovers and trekkers. The lost green valleys that go over uneven paths add to the spirit of adventure.

    The cool breeze and soothing sounds of the waterfall is an amazing experience for all city dwellers. Be sure to take some time out to listen to the chirping of the birds, soak your feet in the cool waters and rejuvenate your soul. Dont miss the Bamboo Chicken served here along with fresh coconut water.

    Location: Near the Borra Caves
    Timings: 24X7

    5. Kailasagiri

    Image Source

    This is a fun and entertainment park in Visakhapatnam and an important tourist attraction. This place is famous for the panoramic view that it presents. There are a large number of fun activities in the park that will keep you engaged. There are provisions for a circular train for children that attracts them and keeps them entertained. There are paragliding facilities which attract a large number of tourists. According to the locals, this is the right place to see the progress of the city over the years.

    Location: Hilltop Park in Visakhapatnam
    Timings: 11 AM – 8 PM

    Suggested Read: 7 Airports In Tamil Nadu For An Awesome Flying Experience In 2022

    6. Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary

    Image Source

    The Kambalakonda wildlife sanctuary is situated on the border of the Visakhapatnam city. It is the house of a large number of animals. The wildlife sanctuary is a beautiful example that shows the harmony existing between the different aspects of nature. The dry seasonal forest and evergreen meadows are a pleasant sight to behold.

    There are various animals to be found here at the sanctuary like the panthers, Sambar deer, and spotted deer, barking deer, jackal, wild boar, and wild dog. This is a must-visit for all nature lovers. It is a sight to behold all these animals, most of which we have never seen except in pictures.

    Location: Outskirts of Vizag
    Entry fee: INR 10 (Adults) | INR 5 (Children)

    7. Borra Caves

    Image Source

    Borra caves are a feast for the eyes. The caves have been formed when the water from the river flows through a limestone area. It chemically weathers the calcium carbonate and the caves are formed. The exquisite variety of speleothems that vary in size as well as the irregularly shaped stalactites and stalagmites will keep you spellbound.

    The hilly terrain, the semi-evergreen deciduous forest washed in the rains and the wild fauna of the Borra Caves are a visual feast the will keep you glued to the place. These caves are to be found at a height of about 705 meters. The sunlight and shadows conjure up dancing shapes within the caves. The natural skylights make up for kaleidoscopic imagery that gives you a surreal feeling. This is one of the best places in Vizag at night.

    Timings: Open till sunset

    8. Simhachalam Temple

    Image Source

    Being the only temple where Shri Varaha Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy is worshipped, this place attracts a lot of tourists and devotees. This place is also one of the very few remaining places in India where the Vaishnavite culture is strictly followed. The Simhachalam temple has a strict prayer routine that remains undisturbed by the influx of tourists.

    The temple itself is extremely beautiful there are intricate stone carvings and designs. The details can be observed from a distance. Throughout the year the deity remains covered in sandalwood paste. It comes to its real form for just 12 hours per year on Akshaya Tritiya. Here one can see Lord Vishnu in the Tribhanga posture where he possesses two hands with the head of a lion on a human torso.

    Timings: Open 24X7

    Frequently Asked Questions About Places To Visit In Vizag In Summer

    Which is the best month to visit Vizag?

    October to March is considered an ideal time to explore the majestic Vizag as the weather is comfortable and pleasing for tourists.

    For which thing Visakhapatnam is famous?

    If you are fond of exploring the shipyards then you must explore the oldest shipyard across India. This is one of the most popular things that Vizag is known for.

    Is it safe to visit Visag during Covid times?

    You need to follow all the mandatory safety guidelines mentioned by the authorities to ensure a safe travel experience. Remain masked while stepping out of the hotel and maintain social distance. Avoid visiting crowded places and keep sanitizing your hands after touching surfaces.

    What are the top attractions to visit in Visakhapatnam?

    There are many popular spots that you can explore in Vizag. Some of the famous attractions that tourists hover on are Ramakrishna beach, Rushikonda beach, VMRDA INS Kursura Submarine Museum, borra caves, tribes India, Simhachalam temple, and dolphin nose.

    What are the most popular things to do in Visakhapatnam with children?

    Vizag has many kid-friendly attractions that the kids will love to explore such as a submarine museum, Kailasagiri, Rishikonda Beach, dolphin nose, Indira Gandhi Zoological Park, Rama Krishna Beach, and Vuda Park. The kids will surely gain knowledge and will have fun discovering all these popular attractions.

    How many days are required for visiting Visakhapatnam?

    You need at least 2-3 days to be able to fully explore Vizag. In this time duration, you should be able to see all the major tourist attractions here as well as try the many activities that this city is famous for.

    Can the tourist spots in Vizag be covered on a cycling trip?

    No, you may not be able to cover the Vizag tourist places on a bicycle. The places to visit in Vizag in summer are not located close to each other. Besides, the hot and humid climate of the city will make it difficult to travel on a two-wheeler where youll be exposed to the harsh rays of the sun at all times.

    What are the other places one can visit in Visakhapatnam?

    Some of the best places to visit in Visakhapatnam other than the ones mentioned above are Dolphins Nose, Matsyadarshini Aquarium, Indira Gandhi Zoological Park, VUDA Park (Taraka Rama), Yarada Beach, among others.

    What is the general budget I should consider while travelling to Visakhapatnam?

    The budget of your trip depends entirely on factors like the season of visit, type of accommodation chosen, type of transport chosen to get around, and much more. If you stay in a budget hotel in Vizag and travel in the offseason, your daily budget may not exceed INR 5,000. However, if you choose to travel in the peak season and pick a 5-star resort for your stay, you may have to shell out as much as INR 20,000 per day.
